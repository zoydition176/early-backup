Customer Background

The client works for an Austria ISP company which focuses on providing internet connectivity and VoIP phone system for over 20 years. He had a big project about 3 long-distance connections and wanted to start this project with an experienced IT solution provider.

What are the Challenges?

“We have a 100 km long fiberpair and want to operate a 40ch DWDM *10gbit (100GHz Grid), yes channel 21 to channel 60. Need amplification as it's not able
to add a new site. We can test a solution without Mux/Demux first.”

  • Long Haul without Relaying

    Utilizing EDFAs for a long distance is necessary,
    question is what kind should be choosed, PA or BA?

  • Non-MUX System

    In the Inception phase, starting without any Mux
    Demux, only using EDFA to extend the link distance.

What Solution has FS Offered?

◆  Current solution, 10G DWDM SFP+ transceivers connect directly

For a large project, deploying a preliminary link without Mux/Demux is a secured and cost-effect choice. We worked a solution which achieved 100km transmission without regeneration sites. The heart of this long-haul solution is our optical low noise amplifier. The DWDM EDFAs amplify the signal power to make sure the light of 80km transcievrs can reach to 100km.

◆  Expansion solution, up to 400G DWDM network with MUX/DEMUX

With the first link's successful running, the customer could safely move to the expansion solution. The 40 channels DWDM Mux/Demux system provides a fast, easy and inexpensive method for adding more wavelengths to existing systems.

  • Long-haul Transmission Achieved

    100KM results a large link loss, choosing a booster amplifier (EDFA-BA) with higher output power guarantees the signal quality for the long-haul transmission at the first stage.

  • Ready for Future

    Considering the increasing demand for business and system capacity, 40CH DWDM MUX solution is provided to meet the expansion needs in the future.
